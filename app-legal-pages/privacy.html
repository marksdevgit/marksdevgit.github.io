Privacy Policy for SuperPaddleHoops

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how SuperPaddleHoops ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

SuperPaddleHoops is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

Game Data Stored Locally

  • Game Progress: Your current level, score, and high score
  • Settings: Your preferences for sound effects and haptic feedback
  • Statistics: Gameplay statistics like total baskets scored

All of this data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and is never transmitted to our servers.

Game Center Data

  • Leaderboards: Your scores submitted to Apple's Game Center (handled entirely by Apple)
  • Achievements: Progress toward game achievements (handled entirely by Apple)

Game Center data is managed by Apple according to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your Game Center profile information.

In-App Purchases

  • Purchase Transactions: We offer optional in-app purchases including "Remove Ads" and tips to support development
  • Payment Processing: All purchase transactions are processed securely by Apple through the App Store
  • Transaction Records: We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information or transaction history
  • Purchase Status: We only track whether you've purchased "Remove Ads" to disable advertisements in the app

Your purchase history and payment information are managed by Apple according to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Advertising

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in SuperPaddleHoops (unless you've purchased "Remove Ads")
  • Ad Personalization: AdMob may collect and process data including device advertising ID, IP address, device information, and app usage data
  • Third-Party Tracking: AdMob uses cookies and similar technologies to deliver and measure ad performance

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Allow Apps to Request to Track). Learn more about Google's data practices: Google Privacy Policy and AdMob Data Usage.

Information We Do NOT Collect

SuperPaddleHoops does NOT directly collect, transmit, or store:

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Analytics or usage data (beyond what AdMob collects for ad serving)
  • Camera or photo library access
  • Contacts or calendar information

Note: While we don't collect this data, Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage information as described in the Third-Party Advertising section above.

How We Use Your Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress between sessions
  • Remember your settings and preferences
  • Display your statistics and high scores
  • Track your current level and paddle size progression

Data Sharing

We do not directly share, sell, or transmit any of your game data to third parties. Your game data stays on your device.

However, the following third parties may collect data as part of their services:

  • Apple: Game Center data and in-app purchase information (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob: Advertising and analytics data as described in the Third-Party Advertising section (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and protected by iOS security features. We do not operate servers or cloud storage for this app.

Children's Privacy

SuperPaddleHoops does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. The app is designed to be safe for users of all ages.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Delete Game Data: Use the app's reset function in Settings to clear your game progress
  • Delete All Data: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
  • Game Center Data: Manage through your iOS Game Center settings
  • Ad Personalization: Control through iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking

Third-Party Services

SuperPaddleHoops uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Game Center: For leaderboards and achievements (see Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Apple In-App Purchases: For processing payments (see Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (see Google's Privacy Policy)

Future Changes

If we add features that collect or use data differently (such as cloud sync or additional analytics), we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy
  • Make it clear what data is collected
  • Provide opt-in mechanisms where appropriate
  • Notify users through app updates
