Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I level up?
A: Score baskets to level up! Each basket advances you to the next level, making the game progressively more challenging.
Q: Why does my paddle get smaller?
A: If you don't score a basket within 10 bounces, your paddle shrinks. Keep scoring to maintain full paddle size! The paddle resets to full size every 10 levels.
Q: What are Paddle Pump and Paddle Damping?
A: These are advanced techniques for controlling the ball:
  • Paddle Pump (swipe up): Adds extra bounce when the ball hits, making it jump higher
  • Paddle Damping (swipe down): Reduces bounce energy when the ball hits, giving you more control
  • Spin Control (fast horizontal swipes): Adds spin to the ball for better trajectory control
Master these techniques to reach higher levels!
Q: How does scoring work?
A: Each basket you score earns you points and advances you to the next level. Your high score is automatically saved and synced to Game Center leaderboards.
Q: What happens to the obstacles/patterns at the top?
A: The sawtooth patterns at the top move and change as you progress through levels, creating different challenges. They're designed to test your ball control skills!
Q: Does my game progress get saved automatically?
A: Yes! Your high score and settings are automatically saved locally on your device. Your scores are also synced to Game Center if you're signed in.
Q: How do I turn off sound or haptics?
A: Tap the Settings button on the home screen to access:
  • üîä Sound Effects - Toggle game sounds on/off
  • üì≥ Haptics - Toggle vibration feedback on/off
Your preferences are saved automatically.
Q: Can I compete with friends?
A: Yes! SuperPaddleHoops integrates with Game Center. Sign in to Game Center to access leaderboards and compete with players worldwide. Your high scores are automatically submitted.
Q: How do I remove ads?
A: You can purchase the "Remove Ads" option from the Settings screen. This is a one-time purchase that permanently removes all advertisements from the game. The purchase is linked to your Apple ID and can be restored on other devices using the "Restore Purchases" button.
Q: What is the Tip Jar?
A: The Tip Jar lets you support the development of SuperPaddleHoops with optional tips. These are completely optional and help us continue improving the game!
Q: I made a purchase but ads are still showing. What should I do?
A: Try these steps:
  1. Force close the app completely and restart it
  2. Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases"
  3. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the purchase
  4. Restart your device if the issue persists
  5. If still not working, contact support (see below)
Q: The app crashes or freezes. What should I do?
A: Try these troubleshooting steps:
  1. Force close the app and restart it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check for app updates in the App Store
  4. Ensure your iOS is up to date
  5. If issues persist, contact support (see below)

Tips for Success

  • Master the timing: Watch the ball's trajectory and position your paddle early
  • Use paddle pump strategically: Swipe up just as the ball hits to reach high baskets
  • Control wild bounces: Use paddle damping (swipe down) to tame unpredictable bounces
  • Add spin for precision: Fast horizontal swipes add spin for better ball control
  • Watch the bounce counter: Keep scoring within 10 bounces to maintain full paddle size
  • Plan ahead: Look at the basket position and plan your bounce trajectory
