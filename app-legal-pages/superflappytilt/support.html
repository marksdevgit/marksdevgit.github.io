Super Flappy Tilt is a tilt-controlled endless flyer! Tap to flap upward through gaps in pipes while tilting your device to move left and right. Each pipe you pass awards taps. Run out of taps and it's game over!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I control Flappy?
A: Tap anywhere to flap upward. Tilt your device left/right to steer horizontally. The bird wraps around the screen edges!
Q: What happens when I run out of taps?
A: When your tap counter reaches zero, it's game over! Pass more pipes to earn taps and stay alive.
Q: How do I get more taps?
A: Each pipe you pass awards 5 taps (3 during rain). The first pipe gives a bonus 20 taps. Nests can restore up to 50 taps!
Q: What triggers the rain?
A: Rain storms start appearing after you pass 75 pipes. They come in random bursts with cooldown periods between.
Q: How do I avoid getting electrocuted?
A: When lightning strikes and pipes glow with electricity, stay in the gaps without touching the pipes. The effect wears off after a few seconds.
Q: What's the deal with the pufferfish?
A: Pufferfish are rare obstacles that appear in pipe gaps when you have lots of taps. Bump into one 3 times to wake it up ‚Äî it'll puff up and fly away! Each bump costs 3 taps.
Q: Does my score get saved?
A: Yes! Your high score is automatically saved locally and submitted to Game Center leaderboards if you're signed in.
Q: How does the scoring system work?
A: Your final score is based on pipes passed, but watching ads at nests comes with a cost. Each ad watched deducts 20 points from your final score. This rewards skilled "pure" runs ‚Äî a player who reaches 500 pipes without ads beats someone who reached 600 pipes using 6 ads (600 - 120 = 480). Your high score and Game Center ranking use this final score.
Q: Can I compete with friends?
A: Yes! Super Flappy Tilt integrates with Game Center. Sign in to access leaderboards and compete with players worldwide.
Q: The app crashes or freezes. What should I do?
A: Try these troubleshooting steps:
  1. Force close the app and restart it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check for app updates in the App Store
  4. Ensure your iOS is up to date
  5. If issues persist, contact support (see below)

Tips for Success

  • Stay centered: The gaps can appear anywhere, so staying near the middle gives you more options
  • Tap conservatively: Don't spam taps ‚Äî each one counts! Gentle, controlled flaps are key
  • Use the wrap-around: Going off one side of the screen puts you on the other ‚Äî use this to dodge obstacles!
  • Watch your tap counter: When it turns red and pulses, you're running low ‚Äî prioritize passing pipes!
  • Rest in nests: Every 50 pipes you'll find a nest. Stay to recover taps before continuing
  • Avoid sitting on pipes: Resting on pipes drains 1 tap per second. Keep moving!
  • Listen for warnings: Audio cues warn you when taps are low or storms are coming
